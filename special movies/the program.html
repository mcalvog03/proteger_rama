The program


is a British-French biographical film about the cyclist Lance Armstrong, directed by Stephen Frears and starring Ben Foster as Armstrong
and Chris O'Dowd as journalist David Walsh. It began filming in October 2013 and was released. on September 23, 2015.
An Irish sports journalist is convinced that cyclist Lance Armstrong's victories in the Tour de France are due to doping.
With this conviction, he begins to investigate and look for evidence that will bring the truth about Armstrong to light.
