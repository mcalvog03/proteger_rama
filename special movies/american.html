American flyers


is a 1985 American sports drama film about bicycle racing directed by John Badham and starring Kevin Costner,
David Marshall Grant, Rae Dawn Chong, Alexandra Paul, Jennifer Grey, Luca Bercovici and Janice Rule. It was written by Steve Tesich.
Marcus Sommers visits his family in St. Louis, Missouri, after being away a long time. Marcus immediately gets into a fight
with his mother over the way she handled the death of his father from a cerebral aneurysm. Marcus asks his brother David to
come back to Madison with him to spend time together.
