Closer to the Edge


The film reveals how, for more than 100 years, riders have come to the Isle of Man to compete. The narration suggests that the TT has always
called for a commitment far beyond any other racing event, and since the race began over 200 people have died in the races.
The second half of the film introduces Bridget Dobbs, mother of two and widow of Paul Dobbs ("Dobsy") who died in the 2010 races.
Bridget understands and accepts that her husband, like many others, died doing something he loved. Despite the near fatal injuries,
the film shows how those that survive all want to go back and do it again.
